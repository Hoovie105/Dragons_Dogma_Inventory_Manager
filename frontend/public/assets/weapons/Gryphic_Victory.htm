                      Gryphic Victory
                      Value 115,200 G
                      Weight 3.26
                      Weapon Type Longsword

                      Gryphic Victory is a Longsword in Dragon's Dogma. Longswords are one of the many Weapon Categories available in Dragon's Dogma. They are exclusively available to Warriors and excel in close-range melee combat interactions. Longswords are the ideal weapon choice for heavier builds and have a greater chance to stagger or knockback opponents when compared to regular Swords. Longswords have their own set of specific Longsword Skills that are especially useful when it comes to crowd control or heavy attacks. Each Longsword varies in stats that can be upgraded to further improve your Build. 

                       

                      A nimble longsword adept both at slicing and stabbing attacks.

                       

                      Where to Find Gryphic Victory in Dragon's Dogma

                      The Gryphic Victory can be obtained from the following sources:

                      • Reward from the quest A Parting Tribute.
                      • Can also be found in a chest on top of the Greatwall during the fight with the two Wights. It has a very low chance to appear however.

                       

                      Dragon's Dogma Gryphic Victory Information

                      Gryphic Victory Base Stats

                      • Strength: 368
                      • Magick: 0
                      • Slash Strength: 100%
                      • Bludgeoning Strength: 0%
                      • Stagger Power: 291
                      • Knockdown Power: 291
                      • Elemental: None
                      • Debilitation Strength: None

                      The Gryphic Victory can be used by the following Vocations:

                      •  Warrior
